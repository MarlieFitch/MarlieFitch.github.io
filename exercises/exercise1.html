About Me

Marlie Fitch is a digital artist, writer, and an English major specializing in creative writing at the University of Massachusetts Amherst. She is passionate in exploring ways to combine her love for art and writing together.

My Story

Marlie transferred to UMass Amherst after recieiving an Associate in Arts in Liberal Arts from Berkshire Community College. Her love for writing creative stories and academic papers shaped her pathway into becoming an English major.

Currently, Marlie works as an intern for UMass's English Department, where she is responsible for revising the department's newsletter. Previously, she also interned as a graphic designer for UMass's Visiting Writers Series, where she designed posters adversting the semester-long event to hundreds of students.

Over the past two years, Marlie has worked on a graphic novel series in her free time, which is nearing the completion of the first volume.
