ABOUT ME

Thanks for visiting my page!

I'm Marlie, a writer and digital artist who recently earned a Bachelor of Arts in English with a concentration in creative writing at the University of Massachusetts Amherst, on top of having an Associate of Arts in Liberal Arts from Berkshire Community College.

I'm eager to combine my passion for art and writing together to create vivid and memorable digital designs. I have experience using software such as Adobe Creative Cloud, Canva, and Clip Studio Paint to create advertisements, book covers, and Illustrations.

If you’re interested in learning about my work, examples can be viewed under the "Portfolio" section.

—Marlie
